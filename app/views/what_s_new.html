Turbo Talk at Turbo International

Our focus on the turbo market through continuous interaction with customers around the world allows Turbo International to better understand market conditions, forecast trends and introduce appropriate solutions. Demands from the worldwide aftermarket for new product and improved services drive our business. In an increasing number of new model applications, the Original Equipment turbo manufacturers do not offer their full product range of assemblies and components to turbo rebuilders. In an effort to satisfy these demands and meet the product shortfall, Turbo International is continuously developing new product and improving the services offered to its worldwide network of customers by aggressively expanding our product range with new product introductions. To keep you informed and up to date with these developments, the latest news, new product offerings, special offers, enhanced customer service and special features we now offer can be found here.
